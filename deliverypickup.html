Delivery & Pickup

Terms & Policies

  1. Delivery Availability

    • Only delivery within Kuala Lumpur and Selangor are available.
    • Delivery times are subject to availability and must be scheduled in advance.

  2. Order Placement

    • Orders for delivery and pickup must be placed two days in advance.
    • Same-day orders may be accommodated based on availability; please contact us directly.
    • Payment for both delivery and pickup orders must be made in full upon ordering.

  3. Delivery Fees

    • A delivery fee will be applied to all delivery orders.
    • Lalamove delivery service will be used for delivery.
    • Fees may vary based on distance and order size; details available upon request.

  4. Delivery Times

    • Deliveries are scheduled during specific time windows.
    • We will do our best to accommodate specific time requests; however, exact delivery times will not be guaranteed.
    • We are not liable for delays or failures in delivery or pickup due to uncontrollable circumstances (e.g., weather conditions, traffic delays)

  5. Pickup Procedures

    • Orders for pickup must be collected during store operating hours.
    • Please specify preferred pickup time when placing order.

  6. Order Changes and Cancellations

    • Changes to orders (including date, time, or items) must be made two days before the scheduled delivery or pickup time.
    • Cancellations made within one day of the scheduled time may incur a cancellation fee.

  7. Recipient Availability

    • For deliveries, please ensure someone is available at the delivery location during the specified time window. We shall not take any responsibility for the item's condition if the delivery attempt failed or item is damaged by the receiver.
    • For failed delivery attempts, additional delivery attempts may be subject to an additional fee.
    • For special delivery instructions, please provide them upon ordering.

  8. Allergies and Dietary Restrictions

    • While we take precautions, please note that out products may contain allergens.
    • Please inform us of any allergies or dietary restrictions when placing order.

Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

Which type of payment methods are accepted?

We accept cash, Visa, Mastercard, TouchnGo, QRPay, and AliPay.


What flavors of cakes do you offer?

We offer a variety of flavors including chocolate, vanilla, strawberry, orange, earl grey, red velvet, matcha, taro, and so on. Please check out our menu for more details.


How far in advance should I place my order?

It is recommended to place orders 48 hours in advance to ensure availability. More notice is required for large cakes.


Can I customize the cake decorations?

Cake decorations can be customized. Please contact us directly to discuss your preferences.


Do you offer gluten-free or vegan cakes?

We offer gluten-free and vegan cake options. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when ordering.


How do I inquire about wedding or special event cakes?

For weddings or special event inquires, please contact us directly to schedule an appointment with our cake designer.


Do you provide cake tastings?

Yes, we provide cake tasting for wedding cakes or large custom orders. Please contact us to schedule a tasting appointment and discuss your preferences.


What are your operating hours?

We are open from Monday to Saturday, 10am to 7pm. Please note that store hours on holidays may vary, so please check our website or call beforehand on holiday hours.
