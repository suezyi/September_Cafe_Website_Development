About September Cafe

A taste of joy in every crumb.

Welcome to September Cafe, a delightful retreat nestled in the bustling heart of Bandar Sunway, Selangor. At September Cafe, we believe that good cake is more than just a dessert - it's a daily ritual of bringing people together and creating memorable moments. Step into our charming space and let the aroma of freshly baked cakes and coffees bring you to a haven of warmth and comfort. We guarantee an indulgent experience you won't soon forget, whether it be a celebration or a simple treat.

September Cafe was established in the year 2024, where a group of students started baking in a dorm. Over the years, we've become more than just a cafe. Not only are each handcrafted cake made with love and passion, but they also serve as the key to countless friendships and memories.

Join us at September Cafe and experience the timeless charm and flavors that made us a local favorite for 10 years. Discover why our dedication to quality, tradition, and genuine hospitality continues to resonate with our guests day after day.

Our Vision

At September Cafe, our vision is to become the go-to destination for cake enthusiasts and community gatherings in Selangor. We aspire to create a welcoming space where people can indulge in the finest cakes, enjoy meaningful moments with loved ones, and feel a sense of belonging. Our mission

Our mission at September Cafe is to craft the most delicious and delightful cakes using the highest quality ingredients and traditional baking techniques. We are dedicated to:
  • Quality:Ensuring every cake is a masterpiece of flavor and texture, made with love and care.
  • Innovation:Continuously experimenting with new flavors and designs to surprise and delight our customers.
  • Community:Creating a warm and inviting atmosphere where everyone feels welcome and valued.
  • Sustainability:Implementing eco-friendly practices in our sourcing, baking, and packaging to minimize our environmental impact.
We strive to make every visit to September Cafe a memorable experience, leaving our customers with a smile and a craving for their next slice.

Our Values

At September Cafe, we hold dear the values of community, quality, creativity, and sustainability. We believe in fostering connections among our guests, ensuring excellence in every cup and dish we serve, embracing innovation to enrich your experience, and promoting eco-friendly practices for a sustainable future. Join us in creating memorable moments over coffee and delicious treats!
